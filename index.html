个人简介

Dongchao Wen (Member, IEEE) received the B.E. and M.E. degrees in signal and information processing from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 2002 and 2005, respectively. He is currently a Principal Researcher with Canon Innovative Solution (Beijing) Co., Ltd. His research interests include computer vision and Trustworthy Deep Learning. He has published over 20 technical papers in IEEE Transactions, CVPR, ICCV, etc., and serves as a reviewer for top international conferences.
