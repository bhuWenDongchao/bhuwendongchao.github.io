Introduction

Dongchao Wen (Member, IEEE) receieved the B.E. degree and M.E. degree in signal and information processing from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 2002 and 2005, respectively. He is currently a Principal Researcher with Canon Innovative Solution (Beijing) Co., Ltd., Beijing, China. His research interests include computer vision (e.g., face recogniton and analysis), and Trustworthy Deep Learning (e.g. adversarial attack/defense). He has published more than 20 technical papers in international journals and conferences, such as IEEE Transactions on Image Processing, IEEE Transactions on Multimedia, CVPR, ICCV, etc. He serves as the reviewer for several international conferences, such as ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, CVPR, etc.
